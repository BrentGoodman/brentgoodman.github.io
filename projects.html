Sonoma Clean Power
Community Choice Aggregation

Project and Contracts Manager
2012 - 2014

Sonoma County, California

More info

Description

Sonoma Clean Power is a community choice aggregator (CCA), one of the first communities to establish a class of public energy providers established by the California Legislature in the aftermath of the early 2000s energy crisis, allowing local communities to create their own alternative to sourcing clean energy. They serve over 250,000 people in Sonoma and Mendocino counties, and the first in California to offer a 100% local renewable energy option.
