Northwestern Pacific Railroad Co. 
Freight Operations

Project and Contracts Manager
2011 - 2015

Northern California
From Marin County to Port of Humboldt

More info

Description

The Northwestern Pacific Railroad is a regional short-line railroad utilizing a 80+ mi stretch of the 271 mile mainline.  N.W.P. Co, Inc. began operations over the section of track between the cities of Sonoma, CA and Windsor, CA. They haul grain for dairy and poultry farms in Sonoma County,  lumber, wine and various other products. At Lombard, the railroad exchanges carloads with California Northern Railroad (CFNR).

Sonoma Clean Power
Community Choice Aggregation

Project and Contracts Manager
2012 - 2014

Sonoma County, California

More info

Description

Sonoma Clean Power is a community choice aggregator (CCA), one of the first communities to establish a class of public energy providers established by the California Legislature in the aftermath of the early 2000s energy crisis, allowing local communities to create their own alternative to sourcing clean energy. They serve over 250,000 people in Sonoma and Mendocino counties, and the first in California to offer a 100% local renewable energy option.
